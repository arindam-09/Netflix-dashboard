1. Title

Netflix Content Insights Dashboard (Country & Time-Based Analysis)

2. Short Description / Purpose

This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Netflix content distribution by country, release year, and content type. It focuses on understanding regional availability, content composition, and trends in movies versus TV shows. The dashboard enables quick exploration of how Netflix content varies across filters like year and country.

3. Used Skills

a. Power BI (Interactive Dashboard Design, Slicers, Data Modeling)
b. DAX (Calculated Measures and Aggregations)
c. Data Cleaning and Transformation (Power Query)
d. Data Visualization and Layout Design
e. Analytical Thinking and Insight Extraction

4. Data Source

a. Public Netflix dataset (Kaggle )


5. Features and Highlights
a. Problem

i. Difficulty in analyzing Netflix content distribution across specific countries and years
ii. Lack of clarity on the proportion of Movies vs TV Shows in selected regions
iii. Limited insight into content ratings and contributor trends (actors/directors)

b. Key Goal

i. Build a filter-driven dashboard for dynamic exploration (by year and country)
ii. Compare Movies vs TV Shows distribution
iii. Analyze content ratings and maturity levels
iv. Understand contributor trends across decades

c. Walkthrough of Key Visuals

i. KPI Cards (Top Left): Displays total count of Movies (60) and TV Shows (24) for the selected filters, giving a quick snapshot

ii. Map Visualization (Top Right): Shows total titles by country, helping identify geographic concentration (e.g., Thailand selected)

iii. Donut Chart (Content Type): Highlights proportion of Movies vs TV Shows, showing Movies dominate the dataset

iv. Rating Distribution (Bar Chart): Displays content ratings (e.g., TV-MA, TV-14), indicating a strong tilt toward mature audiences

v. Trend by Year (Scatter/Line): Compares Movies and TV Shows released in a selected year (2021), showing relative production levels

vi. Directors and Actors by Decade: Bar charts summarizing total contributors, helping understand growth in industry participation

d. Business Impact and Insights

i. Reveals that Movies significantly outnumber TV Shows in the selected region and year
ii. Highlights a strong focus on mature-rated content (TV-MA), indicating target audience preference
iii. Shows regional content concentration, useful for localization and expansion strategies
iv. Contributor analysis (actors/directors) helps understand industry scale and content production trends
